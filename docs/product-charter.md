# Platform Buddy v2 Product Charter

## Core concept

Platform Buddy v2 is a mobile-first training cockpit for powerlifting.

It helps one lifter answer four practical questions:

1. What should I do today?
2. What did I actually do?
3. What does that mean for the next session?
4. Am I ready for the meet?

## Product promise

A calm, fast, competition-aware training companion that turns current strength, RPE, training history, and meet preparation into the next clear action.

## Primary users

- Beginner to intermediate powerlifters
- Lifters training alone
- Lifters learning RPE
- Lifters preparing for their first or next meet
- Coaches who want a simple single-athlete reference flow

## Main surfaces

### HOME

Purpose: decide today's next action.

Must show:

- Today's recommended action
- Current status
- Goal distance
- Wellness state
- Meet countdown if available
- One short Buddy note

### LOG

Purpose: record training quickly.

Must prioritize:

- Lift
- Weight
- Reps
- RPE
- Memo
- Save

No video, VBT, camera, or motion analysis.

### PLAN

Purpose: show today's training prescription.

Must show:

- Today's main lift
- Top set
- Backoff work
- Target RPE
- Adjustment rule

Program configuration is secondary.

### DATA

Purpose: decide the next adjustment.

Must show judgment before charts:

- Recent e1RM trend
- RPE drift
- Completion rate
- Fatigue signal
- Next recommendation

### MEET

Purpose: prepare for competition.

Must show:

- Meet date
- D-day
- Opener candidates
- Checklist
- White-light rules
- Meet notebook

## Non-goals

Platform Buddy v2 is not:

- A VBT app
- A video analysis app
- A camera app
- A motion tracking tool
- A character game
- A broad social fitness app
- A multi-client coaching CRM
- A PL Commentary Bank tool

## Design tone

The UI should feel like:

- A training notebook
- A meet-day checklist
- A rack-side dashboard
- A calm powerlifting cockpit

It should not feel like:

- A gacha game
- A mascot app
- A noisy analytics dashboard
- A video editor
- A lab tool

## Shiba Buddy role

The Shiba Buddy remains as a quiet spotter.

It should support emotion and clarity without controlling layout.

Allowed:

- Small icon
- One concise comment
- Small post-save encouragement
- Calm warning when RPE or wellness suggests caution

## Success criteria

Platform Buddy v2 is successful when:

- LOG is faster than a paper note.
- HOME immediately shows what to do today.
- PLAN is readable during training.
- DATA helps choose the next adjustment without over-reading charts.
- MEET reduces anxiety before competition.
- Shiba Buddy adds warmth without reducing speed.
- VBT/video features are absent from the core app.
